Sabarimala Gold Theft Case

Date: October 17,2025

Pathnamthitta : In a significant development in the Sabarimala gold theft case, Unnikrishnan Potty, identified as the prime accused, was arrested by the Special Investigation Team (SIT) after extensive interrogation. Potty, a Bengaluru-based businessman, had been entrusted with the task of gold-plating temple artifacts at the Sabarimala shrine. However, investigations revealed that he misappropriated approximately 475.9 grams of gold, originally part of the gold-plated covers of the templeâ€™s sacred elements, including the dwarapalaka idols and Sreekovil frames, which were never returned to the Travancore Devaswom Board (TDB) after being handed to a Chennai firm, Smart Creations, for re-plating in 2019. This case has stirred significant public and media attention, raising concerns about corruption and mismanagement within the TDB. The investigation is ongoing, with authorities making notable progress, particularly with Potty's arrest, which suggests deeper institutional involvement.
