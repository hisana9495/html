Emirates SkyCargo Open Operation Center in Kochi

Date: October 17,2025

Kochi: Emirates SkyCargo has inaugurated a new operations centre at Cochin International Airport, Kochi, aiming to significantly strengthen cargo connectivity with the Middle East and Europe. The facility is expected to boost Kochi’s status as a regional cargo hub by enabling faster processing and handling of international shipments, especially from Kerala and Tamil Nadu. With a large storage capacity (reported at around 225,000 metric tonnes), the terminal handled approximately 50,000 metric tonnes of cargo in 2024-25, and Emirates projects a growth of 5-10% in volume over the coming years. The centre also plans to expand services in e-commerce and pharmaceuticals, sectors that have high demand and sensitivity. Officials present at the launch included Emirates SkyCargo manager Ameer Al Zarouni and Cochin International Airport Limited’s managing director S. Suhas. This move is part of broader efforts to reduce pressure on larger airports and distribute cargo operations more evenly,while enhancing export potential from the southern states.
